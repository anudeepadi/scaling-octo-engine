### Installing from the APK File

You should have received an **APK file** in your email. Here's how to install it:

#### Step 1: Download the APK File
1. **Open your email** and find the message with the QuitTxT APK file
2. **Download the APK file** to your phone (it will be named something like "QuitTxT-release.apk")
3. **Remember where you saved it** (usually in your Downloads folder)

#### Step 2: Allow Installation from Unknown Sources
Before installing, you need to allow your phone to install apps from sources other than Google Play:

**For most Android phones:**
1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Security** â†’ **Unknown Sources**
2. **Turn it ON** (you can turn it back off after installation)

**For newer Android phones (Android 8+):**
1. When you try to install the APK, your phone will ask for permission
2. Tap **"Settings"** when prompted
3. **Turn on "Allow from this source"**

#### Step 3: Install the App
1. **Open your file manager** or **Downloads** app
2. **Find the QuitTxT APK file** you downloaded
3. **Tap on the APK file**
4. **Tap "Install"** when prompted
5. **Wait for installation** to complete
6. **Tap "Open"** to launch the app, or find it in your app drawer

#### Step 4: Security Settings (Optional but Recommended)
After installation, you can turn off "Unknown Sources" for security:
1. Go back to **Settings** â†’ **Security**

## ğŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### iPhone/iPad Issues:
- **"Cannot download app"**: Check your internet connection and available storage space
- **TestFlight invitation expired**: Contact our team for a new invitation
- **App won't open**: Try restarting your device

### Android Issues:
- **"App not installed"**: Make sure you enabled "Unknown Sources" and have enough storage space
- **"Parse error"**: The APK file might be corrupted. Try downloading it again
- **Can't find the APK file**: Check your Downloads folder or email attachments

### Account & Authentication Issues:
- **Login problems**: Make sure you're using the correct Google account or provided credentials
- **"iquit" not working**: Make sure you sent it as your very first message after signing in
- **Messages not loading**: Try logging out and logging back in
- **Not receiving responses**: Ensure you started with "iquit" message
